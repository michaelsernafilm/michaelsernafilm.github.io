About

  • 2010-2015

    Education

    I grew up idolizing cinema and what began as a hobby in middle school transformed itself into a career. Mike studied at San Francisco State University where he earned a degree in Broadcast and Communications.

  • 2014-2016

    Live-Streaming

    I began his career working as a producer for a live-stream production company TheCube.com. This eventually led into working on creative content for the company.

  • 2016-2018

    MLB.com/Comcast

    I later took a job at MLB.com as a quality control technician where he would monitor and resolve technical issues with Sony's streaming service, Playstation VUE. After his time with MLB, he sought a more hands-on production workflow with Comcast where he learned how to technical direct, audio mix and work in a live-production truck. Mike also developed his editing and video shooting skills at his time with Comcast.

  • 2018-2020

    Producer at Northern Quest

    I would move to Spokane, WA in the summer of 2018 and took a job as a Video Producer at Northern Quest Resort & Casino. He worked on televison commercials, radio spots and digital content for the casino. He also handeled digital content of over a dozen internal businesses.
